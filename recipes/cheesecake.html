Cheesecake Recipe

Description

This is a rich and creamy cheesecake recipe that's perfect for any special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Add the sugar and beat until well combined.
  4. One at a time, add the eggs and beat until just incorporated.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the mixture into a prepared crust.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the edges are set and the center is slightly jiggly.
