Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a delicious and easy-to-make lasagna recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
