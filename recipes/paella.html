Paella Recipe

Description

This is a traditional Spanish paella recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Chicken broth
  • Saffron
  • Chorizo

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chorizo and cook until browned.
  3. Add the rice and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the saffron and stir for 1 minute.
  5. Gradually add the chicken broth, stirring constantly.
  6. Cover and cook for 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
